Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are self-heating hotpots and how do I use them?

Self-heating hotpots are convenient, portable meals that come with a built-in heating element. To use a self-heating hotpot, follow the instructions on the packaging. Typically, you'll add water to the heating element and wait for it to heat the meal.

2. How should I store self-heating hotpots and other perishable items?

Self-heating hotpots should be stored in a cool, dry place. Snacks and beverages also have storage instructions on their packaging to ensure freshness.

3. How can I find out about new products?

Follow us on social media to stay updated on our latest products and promotions.

4. Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

5. How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times vary by location. Typically, orders are processed within 1-2 business days, and shipping takes an additional 3-7 business days.

6. What is your return policy?

We accept returns of unopened and unused items within 15 days of purchase. Please visit our return policy page for more information.

7. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major debit/credit cards, e-wallets and DuitNow.

8. Can I change or cancel my order?

If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request.

9. Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes, we offer a loyalty program where you can earn points for purchases and redeem them for discounts on future orders.

10. What do I do if my order arrives damaged or incorrect?

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
